Hon Muhammed Ozigis is a Nigerian politician who has had a notable political career. He has held various positions in the government, including serving as a member of the Nigerian House of Representatives representing the Koton-Karfe/Lokoja federal constituency. Ozigis is known for his commitment to improving the lives of his constituents and advocating for good governance in Nigeria. His political career has been characterized by his dedication to public service and his efforts to promote unity and development in his community.

Hon Muhammed Ozigis started his political career in the early 2000s with the Congress for Progressive Change party. He won a seat in the Nigerian House of Representatives representing the Koton-Karfe/Lokoja federal constituency in 2011. Ozigis was an active participant in the legislative process, sponsoring bills that aimed at improving the lives of his constituents, and serving on various committees. He was re-elected in 2019 and continues to advocate for good governance, unity among Nigerians, and the betterment of his constituents. Ozigis' successful political career has earned him recognition and respect for his dedication to public service.
